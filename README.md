💣 Bomb Squad — Worlds 2026 Command Center
FRC Team 16 | Mountain Home, Arkansas
A mobile-first web app built for Team 16's trip to the 2026 FIRST World Championship in Houston, TX (April 28 – May 3, 2026).
🔗 Live App
https://rbergenstock.github.io/worlds-2026/
Open this link on your phone and add it to your Home Screen for one-tap access all week.
iPhone: Open in Safari → Share button → "Add to Home Screen"
Android: Open in Chrome → Three dots → "Add to Home Screen"
📱 What's Inside
Tab	What It Does
Home	Countdown to Worlds, Things to Remember, emergency contacts, tappable location cards for every stop on the trip
Schedule	Full day-by-day itinerary with filter buttons (Tue–Sat), color-coded events, tappable addresses
Matches	Look up any team's match schedule at Worlds using The Blue Alliance API. Preloaded with Teams 16, 3937, 4635, 8044, and 9067
Meals	Restaurant details with menus, phone numbers, addresses, and costs for every meal of the trip
Weather	Live weather for Houston and Mountain Home, interactive radar map, and trip forecast
Scout	Prescouting tool — load any division to see team EPA/OPR stats from TBA and Statbotics
Links	Quick links to TBA, Statbotics, FIRST Events, venue maps, division pages, and more

🏆 About Team 16
FRC Team 16, the Bomb Squad, is a FIRST Robotics Competition team from Mountain Home High School in Mountain Home, Arkansas. We are Hall of Fame inductees and World Champions. Our 2026 robot is Corsair, named after the A-7 Corsair II — an actual A-7 sits on a pedestal at our school on loan from the Navy.
Three Arkansas teams are competing at the 2026 World Championship:

⚙️ Technical Notes
This is a single-file HTML app with no server required. All data is fetched client-side from:
The Blue Alliance API — match schedules, OPR, rankings
Statbotics API — EPA ratings
Open-Meteo API — weather forecasts
RainViewer API — radar imagery via Leaflet.js
Built with the BombsAway Scouting System (v16.3937) — a partnership between Team 16 and Team 3937.
