💣 Who We Are
You represent FRC Team 16 — the Bomb Squad. Hall of Fame. World Champions. 30 years of excellence from Mountain Home, Arkansas. Every person you meet, every interaction you have, reflects on our team, our school, and our community. Carry that legacy with pride.

🤝 Gracious Professionalism
This is the heart of FIRST — competing fiercely while treating everyone with respect. Cheer for great plays even when they're not ours. Help a team that's struggling. Win with humility, lose with grace. The way we treat people matters more than any trophy.

📝 Local Weather Notes

Late April in Houston: expect highs in the mid-80s°F with humidity around 70–80%. The convention center is air-conditioned but walks outside will be warm. Pack light clothing, sunscreen, water bottle, and a light jacket for indoor AC.

PRESCOUTING

📋 Match Watching Instructions

  1. Find their BEST match (highest score) — What are they capable of at peak performance?
  2. Find their WORST match (lowest score) — What do they look like when things go wrong?
  3. Find ONE IN THE MIDDLE — What does a typical match look like for them?
  4. Watch for: drivetrain, auto consistency, cycle times, climb reliability, defense ability
  5. After watching, tap the 📋 button on their card to fill out the scouting form
