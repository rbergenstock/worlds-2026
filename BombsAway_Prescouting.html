🔍 Prescouting — Your homework before Worlds. Step 1: Pick your division below. Step 2: Cards load with EPA, OPR, DPR, rank, record, and win %. Step 3: Sort and filter to find teams worth watching. Step 4: Tap 🔵 TBA to view their matches. After watching, pick your team in the form dropdown and tap GO.

📺 Match Watching Instructions

  1. Find their BEST match (highest score) — what are they capable of at peak?
  2. Find their WORST match (lowest score) — what do they look like when things go wrong?
  3. Find ONE IN THE MIDDLE — what's their typical match like?
  4. Watch for: drivetrain, auto fuel, cycle times, climb level, defense ability
  5. After watching, pick your team in the form dropdown and tap GO to fill out the scouting form
